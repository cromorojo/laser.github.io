⚠️ LASER TATTOO REMOVAL SAFETY CONSIDERATIONS

SKIN TYPES I-II:
• High risk of hypopigmentation
• Start with lowest fluence
• Extended intervals between treatments
• Consider test patch mandatory
SKIN TYPES V-VI:
• High risk of hyperpigmentation
• Use longer wavelengths (1064nm)
• Lower fluences required
• Pre/post treatment skin preparation essential
