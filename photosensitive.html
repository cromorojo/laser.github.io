⚠️ Important Safety Information
The use of photosensitizing medications alone or in combination may cause hyperpigmentation, hypopigmentation, dermatitis, blister formation, or burns. Increased sensitivity to laser procedures may also occur. Always consult with your healthcare provider and perform test treatments when outcomes are uncertain.
